The African Lion

Lions are so closely related to tigers that if you shaved them you wouldn't be able to tell them apart.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Size and Weight: 1.4 - 2 m long and 120 - 191 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 10 - 14 years
  • Habitat: Savanna grasslands

Lions are the most socially inclined of all wild felids, living in groups of related lionesses, their mates, and offspring (called prides). They live in Angola, Botswana, Mozambique, Tanzania, the Central African Republic, South Sudan and other parts of sub-Saharan Africa and wander a territory of 259 square kilometers (according to National Geographic).
